About page

The Dragon Education Society established in 1999, in Bangalore with an ambition to promote and disseminate knowledge and serve in general the cause of education. Our Society has been continuously aiming at developing quality human resources and maintaining high academic standards, covering large segments of the student community.

The relentless pursuit of excellence by Team 1 - Chairman, Dragon Education, has led us to strive for quality in all activities. The Quality Improvement Programs and Support programs have provided the students with intellectual depth and training, necessary to pursue productive careers in their professions.
